# Asset Inventory

This catalog lists the media files and guidelines for replacing them.

## 1. Logos and Brand Assets
* `/logo.png` — High-resolution transparent background brand logo. Used in navigation header and footer.
* `/favicon.svg` — Lightweight vector representation of the brand mark.

## 2. Reusable Visual Placeholders (Replaceable)
* `/company-building.png` — Represents company headquarters (replace with actual building photo).
* `/iso-certificate.png` — Visual preview of ISO certification document (replace with actual certificate image).
* `/company-lab.png` — Illustrates internal laboratory or quality assurance testing setup.

## 3. Product Media
* `/product-photos/*.jpeg` — All product images should follow a consistent aspect ratio (1:1 square) and layout. Use clean lighting and transparent backgrounds.

## 4. Community and Trial Media
* `/community-photos/trial_*.jpeg` — Photo gallery of field operations, trials, and community interactions. Format: 4:3 aspect ratio, landscape orientation.\n
